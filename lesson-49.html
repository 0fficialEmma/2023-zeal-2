CONSEQUENCES OF AN UNGRATEFUL HEART 4:
ETERNITY IN HELL
LESSON 49
4TH AUGUST

BIBLE TEXT: Matthew 27:1-8

MEMORY VERSE: 1 Corinthians 6:9 - "Know ye not that the
unrighteous shall not inherit the kingdom of God?"

CENTRAL TRUTH: Hell is real! Without Jesus, hell is sure!.

OPENING QUESTION: How can ingratitude lead to a loss of blessings?


INTRODUCTION: Ingratitude can lead one to hell fire. You may wonder how. We see
this play out in the life of Judas Iscariot. Rather than being grateful for knowing Jesus,
counting it a privilege to be chosen as one of His disciples, he became ungrateful and sold
Jesus for thirty pieces of silver.

LIFE APPLICATION: What are those things you are doing that can lead to hell fire? Do
you know that your own righteousness cannot save you from hell? Repent today, ask God
for mercy and begin to live a life that pleases Him. Be grateful to Him.

CONCLUSION: Ingratitude is a sin and we have seen from our study today that no sinner will
inherit the Kingdom of God. The Bible records that no matter what God did for the
children of Israel, they constantly grumbled and murmured; many of them died in the
wilderness. How do you think that God would welcome an ungrateful person into His
Kingdom? Let us learn to be grateful always

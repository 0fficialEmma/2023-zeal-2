BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
FAVOUR
LESSON 41
9TH JUNE

BIBLE TEXT: Luke 1:43-55

MEMORY VERSE: Psalm 5:12 - "For thou, LORD, wilt bless the
righteous; with favour wilt thou compass him as with a shield."

CENTRAL TRUTH: God will cover you with favour if you praise Him.

INTRODUCTION: An experience of favour is when God steps in a situation to make a
difference. Favour can be divine - a situation where God singles one out for special
treatment. One of the benefits of gratitude is favour. As God delights in our praises, He
continues to favour us.

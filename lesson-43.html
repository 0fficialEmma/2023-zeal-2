BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
PERFORMANCE OF EXPLOITS
LESSON 43
23RD JUNE

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Samuel 17:1-25

MEMORY VERSE: Daniel 11:32b - "But the people that do know their
God shall be strong, and do exploits."

CENTRAL TRUTH: God created you to do exploits for Him.

OPENING QUESTION: When you hear the name King David, what comes to your
mind?


INTRODUCTION: An exploit is an act done with boldness and intentionality. God
gives us the confidence to do exploits for Him. We all know David to be a man of praise;
he worshipped God so much that God said he was a man after His heart. God made a
covenant with David.

LIFE APPLICATION: Discuss: David was so confident that he would bring Goliath
down and he did. You are God's vessel for exploits! Endeavor to align yourself to Him and
see Him work wonders through you like David.

CONCLUSION: To do exploits for God, we must know God and we can only get to know
Him when we spend time in His presence, speaking to Him in psalms and hymns. When
we spend time in God's presence, we become stronger, more confident and empowered.
When we are strong, we will do exploits for God.

GOD THE SON DELIGHTS IN
A GRATEFUL HEART
LESSON 24
11TH FEBRUARY

BIBLE TEXT: Luke 7:37-48

MEMORY VERSE: Luke 7:44-"And He turned to the woman, and said
unto Simon, Seest thou this woman? I entered into thine house, thou
gavest me no water for my feet: but she hath washed my feet with tears,
and wiped them with the hairs of her head."

CENTRAL TRUTH: A heart full of gratitude will express it without holding back.

INTRODUCTION :It gladdens the heart of Jesus to see believers with a grateful heart.
We see it from the Scriptures as Jesus journeyed from one place to the other performing
signs and wonders by the power of the Holy Spirit that He was glad whenever a person
that was healed expressed gratitude for the healing and restoration received from Him.

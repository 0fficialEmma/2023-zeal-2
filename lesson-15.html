TYPES OF GRATITUDE TOWARDS
GOD 2: VAIN WORSHIP
LESSON 15
10TH DECEMBER

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Samuel 13:4-15

MEMORY VERSE: 1 Peter 4:11 - "If any man speak, let him as
the oracles of God; if any man minister, let him do it as of the ability
which God giveth: that God in all things may be glorified throughs jesus
Christ, to whom be praise and dominion for ever and ever. Amen

CENTRAL TRUTH: Vain worship is done to glorify self, not God.

INTRODUCTION: vain means futile, without result, without yield, unfruitful,
unproductive. Vain worship is the act of worshipping God in an unacceptable manner.

STUDY GUIDE :

A.

WHAT IS VAIN WORSHIP?

Vain worship is offering to God an unacceptable sacrifice or worship - Genesis 4:1-7

Vain worship is any worship not offered unto God in Spirit and in truth 4:23-24.

Vain worship is anything offered unto God grudgingly, unwillingly, compulsion - 2 Corinthians 9:7.

Vain worship is worship offered unto God half heartedly with partial obedience. Samuel 15:19-22.

Be born again; anyone who is not born again can only offer vain worship to God because he or she is not of God - John 3:3.

2.

DISCUSSION: Why are people worshipping God in vain?

LIFE APPLICATION: Whatever we give to God, the intent of the heart matters and He
judges by it. Be it worship, dance offering, or whatever it is we offer to Him, He looks at
the heart behind the action, hence, vain worship to God for whatever reason is
unacceptable to Him.

CONCLUSION: An unbeliever can only offer vain worship to God. Become a child of
God today by accepting Jesus as your Lord and personal Saviour, then you can ensure
that any worship you offer unto God is done in Spirit and in truth.

UNPRECEDENTED WISDOMBENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
PERFORMANCE OF EXPLOITS
LESSON 44
30TH JUNE

BIBLE TEXT: Genesis 41:14-40

MEMORY VERSE: Jeremiah 29:11 - "For I know the thoughts
that I think toward you, saith the LORD, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to
give you an expected end."

CENTRAL TRUTH: God's will and plan for you is to fulfill His
purpose for your life.

OPENING QUESTION: What is God's plan for your life?


INTRODUCTION: You know that God has something good in store for you. You don't
believe He intends you to live a life of pain and sorrow in which each day is a total drag. In
Psalm 63:7, David said "...for you have been my help, and in the shadow of your wings I
will sing for joy." How can you get to that place - where instead of wandering aimlessly
through life, you're actually singing for joy?

DISCUSS: Teenagers to mention and discuss benefits of fulfilling destiny

LIFE APPLICATION: Although God had already settled Joseph's destiny before he was
born, as revealed to him in two dreams, Joseph still cooperated with God to make this
come to pass. How did he do this?

CONCLUSION: God has a special purpose for everyone. His plan for us is that we all live
to fulfill destiny. Living out God's purpose for our lives brings fulfillment and happiness.
When we please God, He blesses us.

REASONS TO BE GRATEFUL TO
GOD 2: RARE GIFT OF THE HOLY SPIRIT
LESSON 17
24TH DECEMBER

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Corinthians 12:1-11

MEMORY VERSE: 1 Corinthians 14:18 - "1 thank my God speak with tongues more than you all.

CENTRAL TRUTH: Give thanks always unto God for the gift of the Holy Spirit.

INTRODUCTION: When Jesus was rounding off His earthly ministry, He promised to
send someone like Himself who would abide in us forever. He kept His Word by giving us
Holy Spirit, the best gift every believer can ever have - James 1:17. Through the Holy
Spirit, the disciples were bold enough to preach the gospel, perform miracles, signs and
wonders that drew many to Christ and even solidified their faith.

Today, we see the manifestation ofthe Holy Spirit in our lives, our walk with God and our
service to Him. It is such that one cannot compare with any human help or assistance and
this is enough to be grateful for.

DISCUSSION: Do you agree that the Holy Spirit is a rare gift gift from God? if you do
why do you think so?

LIFE APPLICATION: There is no way we can please God or carry out any assignment
He gives to us without the presence and help of the Holy Spirit. The Holy Spirit in us as
children of God helps us through the journey of life. Let's be grateful for this rare gift of
His Holy Spirit to us even as we depend on Him daily.

CONCLUSION: The manifestation ofthe rare gifts ofthe Holy Spirit is life changing and
life transforming. It brings hope to the hopeless, life to the dead and strength to the weary.
The joy on the faces of the carriers of this gift is so great. Only a true believer knows this
and will ever be grateful to God for the rare gift of the Holy Spirit. Give your life to Jesus
today to experience this rare gift so you can have something to be truly grateful to God for.

BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
ENJOYING THE PRESENCE OF GOD
LESSON 37
12TH MAY

BIBLE TEXT: Acts 2:1-13

MEMORY VERSE: Psalm 95:2-"Let us come before his presence with
thanksgiving and make a joyful noise unto Him with psalm."

CENTRAL TRUTH: In the presence of God, there is fullness of joy and
blessings; you can access all that when you praise God:

INTRODUCTION: In Psalm 95, we are called to enter God's presence with our sacrifice
of thanksgiving. For the LORD is the great God, the great King above all gods. And Psalm
22:3 makes us understand that God inhabits the praises of His people. And when His
presence comes down, His blessings come along.

STUDY GUIDE

A:)

TWO WAYS TO ACCESS GOD'S PRESENCE

1.

We can enter into God's presence with and through praise.

2.

We can bring down God's presence into a situation through praise.

B:)

6.

His presence will give us direction - Exo 33:14-15.

C:)

HOW CAN I ENJOY THE PRESENCE OF GOD?

1.

Give your life to Jesus as your Lord and personal Saviour.

2.

..................................................- Hab 1:13.

3.

Praise God always and be thankful to Him - Ps 100.

4.

Be in His presence - Ps 23:5.

5.

Create time daily to study God's Word.

CLASS ACTIVITY: Discuss the difference that God's presence has made to your life.
Share your experience.

LIFE APPLICATION: In the journey of life, we cannot do without the presence of God.
Because He owns our lives, as well as our times and seasons, it is therefore important to
seek His presence for protection, direction, provision and blessings.

CONCLUSION: The presence of God and the blessings there of are available daily to as
many as come to Him. In His presence there is fullness of joy, absence of fear and worry,
as well as pleasures forevermore.

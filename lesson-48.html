CONSEQUENCES OF AN UNGRATEFUL HEART 3:
DESTRUCTION (LOSS OF BLESSING & PHYSICAL WELL-BEING)
LESSON 48
28TH JULY

BIBLE TEXT: Numbers 14:1-38

MEMORY VERSE: Proverbs 16:18 - "Pride goeth before destruction,
And an haughty Spirit before a fall."

CENTRAL TRUTH: Having an ungrateful heart is not good. It carries punishments.

INTRODUCTION: The truth about life is that one can lose his or her blessings and
become physically unwell. From our text, the children of Israel were happy to leave Egypt,
to be delivered from slavery but they soon forgot this and grumbled against God. Their
act of ingratitude (grumbling and murmuring) led to them losing their blessings.

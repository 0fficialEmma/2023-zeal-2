BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
SWIFT ANSWERS TO PRAYER
LESSON 33
14TH APRIL

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Samuel 2:1-10

MEMORY VERSE: Isaiah 65:24 - "And it shall come to pass, that before
they call, I will answer; and while they are yet speaking, I will hear."

CENTRAL TRUTH: A grateful heart attracts speedy answers to prayers

INTRODUCTION: To be swift means to be prompt, to be quick and fast, to keep to time.
Sometimes God answers our prayers when the prayer is still a thought in our hearts.
Sometimes He does things before we even ask and sometimes, we may need to wait for a
while to have answers to our prayers, but in all, God is never late.

STUDY GUIDE

A:)

HOW CAN ONE ENJOY SWIFT ANSWERS TO PRAYERS?
To enjoy swift answers to our prayers, we must persistently give God thanks. Hannah was
coming to Shiloh, praying to God for so long for a child of her own and eventually, God
gave her one. After her prayers had been granted, she returned to Shiloh with a grateful
heart and God gave her more children (1 Sam 1:1-28; 1 Sam 2:1-15). Therefore, if you
want instant answers to your prayers, you must have a grateful heart, expressing gratitude
to God.


B:)

EXAMPLES OF PEOPLE IN THE BIBLE WITH GRATEFUL HEARTS,
WHOSE PRAYERS GOD ANSWERED SPEEDILY

1.

Jesus gave thanks before raising Lazarus from the dead - John 11:41-44.

2.

Jesus gave thanks for the multiplication of food to feed 5000 people - Luke 9:16-17.

3.

King Jehoshaphat set up a praise team to praise God and the miracle happened - 2
Chron 20:20-28.

4.

Hannah expressed gratitude to God and the Lord gave her more children
- 1 Sam. 2:1-21.

5.

Paul and Silas were in prison but when they expressed their grateful hearts to God
and prayed, God answered them immediately-Acts 16:22-26.

LIFE APPLICATION: As children, one thing we can give our parents that would make
them happy with us is our gratitude. This week, show them gratitude by saying thank
you. You would discover that they will be happy to do more for you.

CONCLUSION: There is power in gratitude. When we are grateful to God, He is willing
to do more and do it on time. The more we thank Him, the more He answers our prayers
and on and on. It is a beautiful cycle. He loves to receive more gratitude from us. All that
He demands from us and is never tired of receiving, is our praise. He swiftly does things,
so we can praise Him the more.

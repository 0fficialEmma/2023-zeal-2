BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
MULTIPLICATION (DIVINE PROVISION)
LESSON 35
28TH APRIL

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Kings 17:7-16

MEMORY VERSE: 1 Kings 17:14 - "For thus saith the LORD God of
Israel, The barrel of meal shall not waste, neither shall the cruse of oil
fail, until the day that the LORD sendeth rain upon the earth"

CENTRAL TRUTH: Our God is the All-Sufficient God. He can give you anything you want.

INTRODUCTION: Divine provision is a supply that is beyond human understanding -
one that would make one say "It can only be God" Gratitude is acknowledging God's
hand in our lives and as we do this daily, He continues to do more for us. Today, we are
looking at how we can use the key of gratitude to unlock the door to divine provision.

LIFE APPLICATION: Imagine being asked to give thanks for what you have not even
received. To a lay man, it wouldn't make sense but to a Spiritually-guided person who
knows that God is unfathomable, it would.

DISCUSS: God multiplies whatever you give to Him. Discuss this in the light of our topic today.

CONCLUSION: There is power in gratitude. There is an aura that a grateful person
carries that attracts God to them. Try practicing thankfulness on a daily basis and you
would see that the benefits outweigh the amount of time spent giving thanks.

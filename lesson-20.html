HOW TO EXPRESS OUR
GRATITUDE THROUGH GOD 1:
SINGING PRAISE TO GOD
LESSON 20
14TH JANUARY

BIBLE TEXT: Psalm 147:1-20

MEMORY VERSE: - "Praise ye the for Lord: for it is good to
sing praises unto our God; for it is pleasant; and praise is comely."

CENTRAL TRUTH: Singing praises to God is an acceptable way to show our gratitude to Him.

INTRODUCTION: The Yoruba tribe from Nigeria in Africa is one that does not joke
with gratitude, to the extent that they have praise singers, especially in the Kings Palace.
It is said in their adage that anyone who shows gratitude for what was done before will get
more and this habit is instilled into them right from childhood. Even in parties, praise
singing is a custom, especially to the celebrants, to appreciate them for all they have done.

King David in the Bible is one who never joked with praising God, as he never stopped
showing gratitude to God for picking him up as a shepherd boy and raising him to
become King of God's own nation. This made God call him a man after His heart.

LIFE APPLICATION: Take 5 minutes every day to sing a song of praise to God to show
your appreciation for the great and mighty things He has done for you, in you, through
you and in the lives of all your loved ones (Isaiah 42:10). Singing songs of praise to God is a
way of expressing our gratitude to Him.

CONCLUSION: Sing songs of praises to express your appreciation in all things; don't
wait for motivation from anyone. Don't say "my voice is not good", "I can't stay on key',
"people will laugh at my voice".. just sing; sing unto God your Maker and the love of
your soul, making melody in your heart unto the Lord. He deserves all of your praises
make it a duty to sing unto Him always (Psalm 145: 1-3).

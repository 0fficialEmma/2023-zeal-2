CONSEQUENCES OF AN UNGRATEFUL HEART 2:
UNFRUITFULNESS
LESSON 47
21ST JULY

BIBLE TEXT: 2 Samuel 6:14-23

MEMORY VERSE: John 15:6 - "If a man abide not in me, he is cast
forth as a branch, and is withered; and men gather them, and cast them
into the fire, and they are burned."

CENTRAL TRUTH: Avoid being unfruitful by always being grateful to God.

INTRODUCTION: Our text today is about a woman called Michal - the daughter of
Saul. Michal was David's wife. When the ark of God was brought into the city of David
and David was dancing before the Lord with all his might, the Bible recorded that when
Michal saw him, she despised him in her heart and even expressed this to David (Vs 20).
As a result, Michal had no children to the day of her death. To despise someone is a sign of
ingratitude, being unappreciative of him or her. In this situation, while David was
showing gratitude, Michal was displaying ingratitude.

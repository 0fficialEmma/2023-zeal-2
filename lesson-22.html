HOW TO EXPRESS OUR GRATITUDE
THROUGH 3: DANCING AND BODY LANGUAGE
LESSON 22
14TH JANUARY

BIBLE TEXT: 2 Samuel 6:14-22

MEMORY VERSE: Psalm 150:4 - "Praise him with the timbrel and dance
praise him with stringed instruments and organs."

CENTRAL TRUTH: Dancing and body language is a way of expressing
our gratitude to God.

INTRODUCTION: One of the ways by which a person shows happiness, especially after
getting something he or she had been yearning for, is to shout and dance for joy, even if the
person does not know how to dance well. You would see such just move his or her body in
any way, even if it does not match the rhythm of the music. Often times, the greater the
news, the more the dance and celebration.

David is a perfect example in the Bible. Despite the fact that he was a king and much-
decorated war lord who had achieved great feats, he threw caution to the wind and forgot
about modesty when he was praising God, to the extent that he danced in public to show
that he was grateful to God for His goodness in securing victory for him (2 Sam 6:14-22).

God has secured many victories for you, hence, you are to show that your appreciation by
moving your body for Him.

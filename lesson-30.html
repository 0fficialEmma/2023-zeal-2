BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
UNSPEAKABLE JOY
LESSON 30
24TH MARCH

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Samuel 2:1-10

MEMORY VERSE: Romans 14:17 - "For the kingdom of God is not
meat and drink; but righteousness, and peace, and joy in the Holy Ghost.

CENTRAL TRUTH: Be grateful for you to experience and enjoy unspeakable joy.

INTRODUCTION: As we continue to learn about the benefits of a grateful heart, today
we want to look at unspeakable joy as one of the benefits of having a thankful heart. When
something is unspeakable, it means it is beyond expression, it cannot be comprehended,
it surpasses one's understanding. Imagine you having joy that is unspeakable. You would
just be bubbling everywhere you go with smiles. Our study today is about accessing this
joy using the key of gratitude.

LIFE APPLICATION: This week, try to be grateful for everything that happens to you.
good or bad and you will see that your joy will be full throughout.

CONCLUSION: Hannah had every reason to be upset with everyone around her when
she did not have a child, but she chose to talk to God. When God gave her Samuel, she did
not say "only one?" Rather she came back to Shiloh with songs of gratitude and the Bible
says God gave her six more children. Hannah's joy then became unspeakable.

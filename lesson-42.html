BENEFITS OF A GRATEFUL HEART:
UNPRECEDENTED WISDOM
LESSON 42
16TH JUNE

BIBLE TEXT: 1 Kings 3:1-15

MEMORY VERSE: 1 Kings 4:29 - "And God gave Solomon wisdom
and understanding exceeding much, and largeness of heart, even as the
sand that is on the sea shore.''

CENTRAL TRUTH: God gives the wisdom from above that surpasses
human or worldly understanding.

OPENING QUESTION: Is there wisdom in being a grateful person?


INTRODUCTION: A simple definition of wisdom is that it is the correct application of
knowledge. The word unprecedented means unequalled or unparalleled. Therefore, our
topic is about having the kind of wisdom that is divine and can't be matched with others.
One can be knowledgeable about something but until you apply that knowledge, you are
not seen as a wise person. We have been learning about gratitude for so long and after all
that has been taught, if you remain ungrateful, then you are not wise.

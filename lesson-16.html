REASONS TO BE GRATEFUL TO
GOD 1: GOD'S UNCONDITIONAL LOVE
LESSON 16
17TH DECEMBER

BIBLE TEXT: Lamentation 3:22-23

MEMORY VERSE: John 15:13 "Greater love hath no man than this,
that a man lay down his life for his friends."

CENTRAL TRUTH: We are to be grateful to God for
unconditional love towards us.

INTRODUCTION: Have you ever looked at the story ofJesus on the cross of calvary as
He was about to die for the sins of mankind, when the thief beside Him asked to be
remembered and Jesus responded that he would be with Him in paradise? The thief
didn't do anything to receive God's love; he simply believed that Jesus was God, that He
could save him and Jesus did just that.

STUDY GUIDE :

A.

UNCONDITIONAL LOVE
Unconditional love is love without any expectations, limitations or strings attached, It is
not based on anything done in return, rather, it's freely given without the expectation
anything in return.

A.

WHAT HAS GOD'S UNCONDITIONAL LOVE BROUGHT TO US?

Salvation -John S: 16, Ephesians 2:8 4:23-24.

.....................................- 1 John4:18.

It covers a multitude of sins - 1 Peter 4:8

....................................... -John 1:12, I John 3:1

We are strengthened to replicate the same to others -1 John 3:16.

............................................ - 1 Peter 2:24.

ACTIVITY: Class Discussion - Using 1 Corinthians 13: 1-13, describe God's love for us.

LIFE APPLICATION: God's unconditional love motivated the plan for salvation and as
such we have to be grateful. Receive God's unconditional love it is the foundation
of your relationship with Him. Only those who have a relationship with God can
experience and enjoy this unconditional love. Give your life to to partake of this
unconditional love for which you will forever be grateful to God

CONCLUSION: Unconditional love does not mean God loves everything we do, but
rather, that His love is so intense that He loves every sinner, no matter how bad such may
be in his sin, He still makes a way for such to find love, life and holiness. There are a lot of
benefits in God's unconditional love towards us, hence we have a lot of reasons to be
grateful to Him.
